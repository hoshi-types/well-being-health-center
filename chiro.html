    What is Chiropractic?

    Chiropractic is a healthcare method that works on the spine and the nervous system so that the body can feel and function properly. Chiropractic works by detecting and correcting spinal misalignments so that the Nervous System can function properly. Injuries, stress, illness, and/or repetitive motion can cause misalignments.

    If the misalignment interferes with the spinal cord or nerves, it will result in pain or malfunction some-where in the body. Chiropractic care is safe, gentle and effective. Its benefits go far beyond relieving back and neck pain and can help people of all ages from infants to the elderly.
